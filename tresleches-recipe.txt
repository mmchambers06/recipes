1 box of yellow cake mix
1 tub of cool whip
1 13.4 oz of evaporated milk
4 oz. sour cream
1 12 oz can of condensed milk
Ground cinnamon, to taste

prepare cake mix in a 9x11" pan per box's directions
let cool for 10 min
while cooling, blend together evap milk, condensed milk and sour cream until well combined
use a fork to poke holes all throughout the top of the cake
pour blended mixture over cake, letting it soak in evenly
refrigerate at least an hour
top with cool whip, sprinkly cinnamon on top
enjoy!
